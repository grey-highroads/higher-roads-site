Sample Build

Meet MindBloom

MindBloom is a demonstration build showing how a Visual Product World works across motion, stills, and channel assets. The brand is a functional beverage line built on adaptogens, nootropics, and electrolytes for people who care more about long-term health than quick fixes. The brand's visual world is deep forest green and aged gold, drawn from rugged outdoor landscapes and the conviction that the best things take time. The visual challenge: make a wellness beverage feel as grounded and purposeful as the philosophy behind it.

Pixel perfect product renders, placed in an extended brand world.

The Craft Behind the Build

Jailhouse Brewing

A Visual Product World has depth because it is built by people who work at this level. Jonathan Richter developed the complete visual identity for Jailhouse Brewing, a craft brewery operating out of a converted jailhouse in Hampton, Georgia. The brief called for imagery with the weight of the place: weathered, specific, and rooted in the building's own mythology. He designed and illustrated the full label system, each beer earning its own persona from the character of the building. The Justice Series went further: judge, jury, jailer, and executioner, rendered at 12 feet in charcoal for the Solitary Confinement release.
