A functional mushroom beverage brand needed their launch event to feel like a world, not a party. We had five days to make that happen.

Client Mycopop Functional Energy
Type Brand Activation · Projection Mapping
Timeline 5 Days
Attendance 100+ Guests
The Ask

Mycopop came to us with a brief and a deadline. They were activating their functional mushroom energy drink at Culture Club — a community event in Nashville bringing together food, art, music, and local vendors. The room needed to do more than look good. It needed to feel like the brand.

The Challenge

Five days from brief to live event. The space had multiple walls, surfaces at different heights, and an existing HVAC unit that wasn't going anywhere. Everything from room geometry, to content, to calibration had to be resolved before the doors opened. No room for iteration on the night.

The Result

The room was fully mapped and live by event night. Over 100 guests walked into a space that moved. Projection across three walls, a playable retro video game wall branded to Mycopop, a panel backdrop, and animated content loops, all built around the brand's retro gaming identity.

What We Built
01 Full Room Projection

Three walls mapped and live with branded motion content. Mycopop's retro gaming aesthetic translated into a moving environment that surrounded guests from every angle.

02 Playable Video Game Wall

A custom-branded arcade game projected wall-scale, playable by guests. The mascot, the product, the brand palette, all inside a working game built for the event.

03 Panel Backdrop

A dedicated projection surface for the evening's panel discussion, featuring product logos and cans animated across the wall, creating a photo-ready branded background without any physical build.

04 Content Production

All 3D animation and motion content produced in-house. The low-poly mushroom mascot, animated cans, retro environments, and color sequences were all made from scratch for this space.
