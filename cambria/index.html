03
Visual directions

Three directions. One space.

Content is designed seasonally or thematically. These are three starting points — each can be adapted, evolved, or combined with a brand partner's identity.

Organic
Natural / Botanical

Living textures, slow-moving flora, deep forest atmospheres. Warm and grounded. Works well for wellness brands, spring programming, or intimate evening events.

Electric
Electric / Contemporary

High contrast, graphic, kinetic. Deep indigo and violet fields with sharp light trails. Fits brand activations, product launches, and events with a younger, design-forward audience.

Ember
Ember / Warm Dark

Deep amber, burnt orange, and slow fire movement. Cinematic and indulgent. Made for whiskey and spirit brands, late-night programming, and CMA-adjacent events in Nashville.
