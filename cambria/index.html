03
Now put it to work

Three ways the space earns its keep.

The same projection infrastructure supports three distinct modes. Each has its own content, its own purpose, and its own commercial logic.

Evergreen

The Rux brand loop. Runs any night the bar is open. Gives the space a visual identity guests notice without it demanding their attention.

Event Nights

Tastings, trivia, live music, seasonal programming. The visuals shift to match the occasion and give guests a reason to feel like they're somewhere special.

Brand Takeover

A corporate client or brand partner books the rooftop. The room becomes theirs for the night. Full visual ownership, built around their identity and what they're there to sell.
