From Space to Screen
01 Capture the Space

We photograph and measure every surface, angle, and architectural detail. From that, we build a precise 3D model of the environment. The foundation everything else is built on.

02 Map the Surfaces

Using the 3D model, we define every projection surface down to the inch. Throw distances, overlap zones, geometry corrections. All resolved before a single frame of content is made.

03 Make it Move

With the space fully understood, we design and produce content that fits it exactly. Video, animation, generative loops, or layered stills. Whatever the moment calls for, built to land where it's supposed to.

How we work

Immersive work starts with understanding the space, the architecture, the audience, and the moment you're designing for. A hotel bar is not a music venue. A brand activation is not a museum install.

We handle the full chain: content design, projection geometry, hardware specification, and on-site calibration. You don't need to know how any of it works. You need to know what you want people to feel.

Immersive work and experiential marketing often overlap, but they're not the same thing. We don't lead staffing, permits, sampling, or tour logistics. We create the visual experience layer: the part people see, feel, remember, and post.
